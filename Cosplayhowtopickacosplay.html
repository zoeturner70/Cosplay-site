How to pick a Cosplay

Choosing a character for a cosplay is the first step, for some this comes easy and others may find it a challenge. It's important to to pick a character that you admire and/or connect with from a TV show, movie or game. If you choose a character you love, the process of making and wearing the costume will feel even more enjoyable. Another crucial point is to consider the costume's complexity, your skills and budget, as even though it's good to challenge yourself, it's even more important to be realistic. Some popular cosplay characters include superheroes like Wonder Woman and Spider-Man, characters from anime such as Naruto and Sailor Moon, and iconic figures like Harley Quinn and Jack Sparrow. Choose a character that resonates with you and have an enjoy the experience!
