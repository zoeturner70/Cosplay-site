How to get into Cosplay!

Convention guide

Some popular UK conventions include:

  • MCM London Comic Con
  • EGX Birmingham/London
  • London Film & Comic Con
  • Megacon Live
  • Glasgow Comic Con
  • Portsmouth Comic Con

Attending your first comic con can be daunting especially if you are going in cosplay, but with planning and preparation, you will have an amazing time socialising with like minded people about your favourite things! Planning ahead and checking the schedule is vital to ensure you don’t miss out on any panels or activities you’re interested. Conventions, like comic con, often have a large and diverse amount of panels, activities and shows that are definitely worth checking out so make sure you plan accordingly! On the day, you’ll be doing your fair share of walking around so make sure you wear suitable footwear! And if your cosplay doesn’t allow for that, then bring a spare just in case. There are many amazing food vendors to try and stalls that offer some really cool stuff, but they don’t always take card so make sure to bring some cash along. And consider bringing a backpack to carry any of this cool merchandise you may buy. And finally, don’t forget to bring a water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the day and have a blast at comic con!
